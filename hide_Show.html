Home | Shuttle

For decades, transportation has been a huge problem for the women of Bangladesh. Shuttle is an app based pick and drop service for women that aims to solve this problem. With verified and responsible trip managers and a hard-working team, we work relentlessly to ensure safe travel for women with the highest comfort possible.
